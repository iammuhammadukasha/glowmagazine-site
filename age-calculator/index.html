Frequently Asked Questions

How does the age calculator work?
Enter your date of birth and an optional target date. The tool counts the precise years, months and days between the two, accounting for leap years.
Can I calculate age on a future or past date?
Yes — change the "age at date" field to any date and the calculator shows how old you were, or will be, on that day.
