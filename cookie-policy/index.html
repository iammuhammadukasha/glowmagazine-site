Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Glow Magazine uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit glowmagazine.com.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember preferences and understand how visitors use the site.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand traffic and improve the site
  • Advertising cookies — used by ad partners (e.g. Google AdSense) to show relevant ads and measure performance

Third-party cookies

Advertising and analytics partners may set their own cookies when you visit our site. We do not control these cookies. Refer to Google's policies for AdSense-related cookies.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect site functionality. For Google ad personalisation, visit Google Ads Settings.

More information

See our Privacy Policy for how we handle personal data. Questions? Contact us.
