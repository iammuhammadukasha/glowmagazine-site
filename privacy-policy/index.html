Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Glow Magazine ("we", "us", "our") operates glowmagazine.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website and use our free tools.

Information we collect

Automatically collected data: Like most websites, we may collect standard log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL) through our hosting and analytics providers.

Your rights

Children

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
